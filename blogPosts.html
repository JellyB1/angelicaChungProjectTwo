New activities

Monday January 30, 2019 by Saron

Follow the lay of the land. It's most important. When things happen - enjoy them. They're little gifts. Let's make some happy little clouds in our world. Every day I learn. Automatically, all of these beautiful, beautiful things will happen. Put it in, leave it alone.

The light is your friend. Preserve it. You create the dream - then you bring it into your world. Making all those little fluffies that live in the clouds. I think there's an artist hidden in the bottom of every single one of us.

Didn't you know you had that much power? You can move mountains. You can do anything. But we're not there yet, so we don't need to worry about it. All you have to learn here is how to have fun. We'll take a little bit of Van Dyke Brown. So often we avoid running water, and running water is a lot of fun. Talent is a pursued interest. That is to say, anything you practice you can do.

We touch the canvas, the canvas takes what it wants. This present moment is perfect simply due to the fact you're experiencing it. This is where you take out all your hostilities and frustrations. It's better than kicking the puppy dog around and all that so. Let your heart be your guide. Play with the angles.
